Welcome to Save The Place 🏯 🏰 🏫

    Save The Place is a Progessive Web App. It helps you to save the wonderful places that you've been to and would like to share, using these features:
  • Of course, marking your location
  • Taking a photo with your camera, or
  • Uploading an existed photo from your photo album, if you don't allow camera access
  • Deleting photo if you think you can take a better photo 😜
  • Installing the Web App onto your Desktop/ Home Screen
  • Saving the place also when you have no internet connection
